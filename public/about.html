Our Story

CatManager was born from a simple passion: the love of cats and organization. We noticed there was a lack of elegant solutions for cat lovers who want to keep track of their favorite companions, their unique characteristics and their little habits.

What started as a small personal project has grown into a real platform, carefully designed to offer the best possible user experience.
